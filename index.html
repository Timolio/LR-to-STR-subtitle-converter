How it works

                

                    

                        
01

                        

                            Export from Language Reactor
                        

                        

                            In the Language Reactor (formerly LLN) extension,
                            open an episode and click the export subtitles
                            button — your browser will save an .html file.
                        

                    

                    

                        
02

                        
Drop it here

                        

                            Drag the file into the upload zone. If needed,
                            adjust the timing offset with the + and − buttons.
                        

                    

                    

                        
03

                        
Download your SRT

                        

                            Hit the button and the subtitle file saves to your
                            computer. Works in Migaku, VLC, MPV, and any other
                            media player.
